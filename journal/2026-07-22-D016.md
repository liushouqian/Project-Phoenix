## Technical understanding

Before this task:
I thought error handling was mainly about catching exceptions.

After this task:
I understand that different layers have different responsibilities.

Architecture:

Route Layer:
- Handle HTTP request/response.

Service Layer:
- Handle business logic.
- Raise meaningful exceptions.

LLM Layer:
- Communicate with external AI services.
